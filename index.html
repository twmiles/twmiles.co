About me

I’m Tracy Miles, an Infrastructure Security Engineer specializing in securing identities, cloud, and critical infrastructure at scale.

At Société Générale, I’ve administered CyberArk PAM for 200,000+ privileged accounts across North America and Europe, automated HashiCorp Vault deployments to improve CI/CD reliability, and supported enterprise security platforms including Qualys, Symantec DLP, and Imperva. My work spans IAM policy design in AWS and Azure, compliance with frameworks like NIST, PCI-DSS, and ISO 27001, as well as adhering to supervisory guidance from the ECB and U.S. Federal Reserve / OCC. I’ve also led 20+ disaster recovery exercises and developed playbooks for critical business applications.

I’m currently pursuing an M.S. in Cybersecurity at NYU, focusing on cloud security, AI governance, and applied cryptography, and I build security-minded projects like PassMeter (a password entropy analyzer) and the Potion Programming Platform, a platform to teach kids to code while keeping their data safe.
